About

Narrated by Jack Dorsey from the award winning series ‘Earth Sources’ comes the ultimate portrait of the earth’s energy consumption. This documentary conveys the use of energy today and also highlights the worlds demand for energy in 2020 and what effect this will have on the earth in the long run.

The client is a film maker and the audience for the DVD will be people interested in the world’s demand for energy in 2020 and to the uneducated about the topic to give them some more insight. The style of cover I will be designing will be an eye-catching design that clearly tells the viewer of the design about what the DVD is about.
